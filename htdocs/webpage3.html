Matter

Matter is anything that has mass and volume

  • Mass is the amount of matter in a substance. It is different from weight which is the pull of gravity. A matter could be small in size but huge in mass.
  • Volume is the amount of space that a matter occupies. Small and big are descriptions of volume.

Phases of Matter

The most common phases phases of matter are solid, liquid and gas

  • Solids has a definite shape and volume. Its molecules are tightly packed together.

  • Liquids takes the shape of their container and has loosely packed molecules

  • Gas have no definite shape and has loosely packed molecules

Summary

Matter is anything around you. It has 3 phases named solid, liquid and gas.
