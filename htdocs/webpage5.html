What is Force?

Force is any push or pull. It is not limited to push or pull made by physical contact, gravity is also a force. Force is measured in newtons.

Types of Forces

  • Gravitational Force is the force that makes things go downward. Everything has gravity but much massive objects have a much greater gravitational force.

  • Normal Force is the force exerted by surface. When you lean against a wall, the wall also exerts normal force onto you.

  • Friction is the force that stops movement across surfaces.

  • Tension is the force exerted by a string, wire, cable etc.

  • Applied Force is the force exerted by an object onto another object.
